Our Story

Three generations of culinary excellence, blending the bold flavors of Thailand with the refined techniques of Chinese cuisine.

A Legacy of Flavor

Founded in 1985 by Master Chef Chen and his Thai wife Mali, Ocean Dragon began as a small family restaurant with a simple vision: to create the perfect harmony between Thai boldness and Chinese elegance in every seafood dish.

Today, we continue this tradition using only the freshest catch from local fishermen, combined with authentic spices sourced directly from Thailand and China. Our commitment to quality and tradition has made us the premier destination for seafood lovers.
